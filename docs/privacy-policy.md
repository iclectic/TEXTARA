# Textara Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-06-23

Textara is a private, offline-first reader for DRM-free EPUB and PDF files.

## Data Collection

Textara does not require an account and does not include advertising, analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

## Data Stored On Device

Textara stores the following data locally on your device:

- Imported book metadata such as title, author, file type, reading progress, and date added.
- Reader settings such as theme, font size, margins, line height, and accessibility preferences.
- Bookmarks, highlights, and notes you create.
- Backup files only when you explicitly export them.

## Book Files

Imported EPUB and PDF files remain on your device. Textara does not upload your books to a server.

## Backups And Exports

When you export backups, highlights, notes, Markdown, PDF, or JSON files, those files are saved to local device storage or to a destination you choose through Android system file pickers. You control where exported files are stored and shared.

## Permissions

Textara uses Android system file pickers to let you choose EPUB, PDF, backup, and export locations. The app should not request broad storage access unless a future release explicitly documents why it is needed.

## Third Parties

Textara currently does not send personal data to third-party services.
